Charles Lyell's visual data relationship is a case study that investigates and attempts to visualise Charles Lyell's personal relationships and their scientific impact based on data from the return address and sender, as well as some annotations from previous owners and archives of Charles Lyell's personal correspondence, both family and professional, found in the archives of the University of Edinburgh.


In this resource, you can explore the scientific debates and theories that circulated in Charles Lyell's personal circle during his time and learn about many subjects.
